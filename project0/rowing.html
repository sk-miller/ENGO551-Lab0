Rowing!
The activity that gets me to voluntarily leave my bed at 5:30 am every morning.
How Long Have I Been Rowing?
I began rowing in November 2019, in my first semester of university. All throughout high school, I had been a competitive swimmer and I had planned continue this is university, but when I got to U of C I realized it wasn't really possible with a full time engineering schedule. After a few weeks of down time, one of my friends suggested I join the U of C Rowing Team, and I haven't looked back since.
What Is My Greatest Accomplishment in Rowing?
Over the years, I've been able to participate in numerous regattas and events. In this time, I think my greatest accomplishment was placing 2nd of 38 in the Women's Collegiate 8+ at the Head of the Charles Regatta in Boston, MA.
