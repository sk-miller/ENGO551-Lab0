Why Did I Choose Civil Engineering?
I chose to complete a degree in civil engineering because I love learning about the built world around me. Over the course of my degree I have had the opportunity to learn about a wide variety of topics such as airport runway design, flood mapping/prediction, water treatment, bridge design and so much more. After graduation, I plan to work in water resources, creating models to predict future flows and water levels.
Why Am I Working Towards a Minor in Digital Engineering?
I enjoyed ENGG233 but ultimately did not want to become a software engineer. I figured the minor would provide me with an oppurtunity to still learn about a variety of software/comp-sci topics
